Our Story

Norel's Café is more than a coffee shop; it is the culmination of a lifelong personal dream and a dedication to the craft. Named for and founded by me, John Norel M. Cabubas, this venture started not in a commercial kitchen, but with a simple, genuine passion for exceptional coffee and fresh-baked goods, nurtured right in my home.

We source local beans, bake daily, and keep our community at the heart of every cup. Drop by and taste the warmth!
